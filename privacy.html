Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 13, 2026

Welcome to Neon Dash. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle information when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect Automatically

Neon Dash is built to be a privacy-first game. We do not require account registration. However, we use third-party services that automatically collect technical data to function:

  • Device Information: Operating system, device model, and unique identifiers (like Android Advertising ID).
  • Usage Data: High scores, game progress, and ad interactions (stored locally on your device).
  • Technical Logs: IP addresses (used for general location and security) and crash reports.

2. Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may use your device's advertising ID to show personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized ads in your Android device settings.

For more details, see Google's Privacy & Terms.

3. Data Storage

Your game data (like your Best Score) is stored locally on your device using Local Storage. We do not host your personal data on our own servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Neon Dash is a general audience game. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has shared data with us, please contact us immediately.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in Europe or CCPA in California), you have the right to access, delete, or limit the use of your data. Since we do not store personal profiles, you can "reset" your data by clearing the app's cache or uninstalling the game.
