Corrected Privacy Policy for Neon Dash

Effective Date: February 16, 2026

Welcome to Neon Dash. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle information and data security when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect and Share

Neon Dash does not require email registration. However, we collect the following:

  • User Nicknames: We collect the unique nickname you choose for the Global Leaderboard to display your rank and scores to other players.
  • Device Identifiers: Shared with AdMob for relevant ads.
  • Approximate Location: Used for localized ads.
  • App Activity & Performance: High scores and crash logs to improve gameplay.

2. Data Security and Encryption

We take your data security seriously. All data, including leaderboard nicknames, is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).

3. Third-Party Advertising (AdMob)

This app uses Google AdMob. You can opt-out via your device's "Google Settings > Ads" menu.

4. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Best scores and preferences are stored on your device.
  • Server Storage: Your Nickname and High Scores are stored on our secure database to enable the leaderboard. You can request deletion of this data by emailing us below.

5. Children's Privacy

Neon Dash is a general audience game. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Data Deletion Requests

If you want to delete your data from our servers, you can request deletion by sending an email to bela.apps10@gmail.com with your request including your Name. We will process your deletion request and remove your leaderboard data from our database.

7. Contact Us

Email: bela.apps10@gmail.com
